History


We are Team two and our business started in Danville, PA from the glorious minds of Alex Myers, Robert Shea, Michael Palmer, Kristen Morse, and Jessica Kent in 2017. We will be making a gaming website that has its own mobile app and webserver with everything loaded onto it with extensive databases. It is a small business but we hope to dominate the market after we get everything up and running! We need our own server and website, as well as a mobile app. We need databases that are dedicated to each and every video game. We will be updating the information as time goes by and new games are released. We will be keeping up with the older games as well to be sure that our users will be satisfied with both old and new games.
